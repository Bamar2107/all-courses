there are different kinds of padding avliable in bootstrap

padding(p), padding-left(pl), padding-right(pr), padding-top(pt), padding-botto(pb)

padding ranges from 0 to 5

  • 0 means 0rem
  • 1 means 0.25rem
  • 2 means 0.5 rem
  • 3 means 1rem
  • 4 means 1.5rem
  • 5 means 3rem

by default there is 15px of padding from left & right side.
