there are many properties of tooltip() method like animation (default value is true), delay :{"show":1000, "hide" :1000}, trigger offset etc.

Once user click on the dispose button then tooltip message will be deleted and hover will not work. To restore the message again either refresh the page or click on toggle or show button.

enable & disable are the other two methods. difference between disabled and dispose is disabled just hide the content while dispose completely delete the tooltip content

toggleEnabled is the another method which is the combination of enable & disable
